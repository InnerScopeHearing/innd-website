Business highlights

Proof points from three generations of hearing-care operating expertise.

HearingAssist on the Walmart shelf

Sold through Walmart for years, with branded in-store displays across more than 750 Walmart locations, and distribution that expanded into CVS, Rite Aid, and Walgreens.

iHEAR Matrix in national retail

iHEAR Matrix Rechargeable over-the-counter hearing aids launched into Walmart's nationwide over-the-counter hearing aid display in 4,218 stores in November 2023.

The iHEARtest at-home hearing screener

An app-based, at-home hearing screening tool from the iHEAR technology base, part of the teleaudiology and remote-fitting infrastructure.

Multi-retailer and pharmacy reach

Across the iHEAR and HearingAssist brands, products have reached consumers through Walmart, CVS, Rite Aid, and Walgreens, alongside OTCHealthMart.com.
