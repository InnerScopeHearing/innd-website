        Market opportunity

        A massive, underserved market — and a regulatory tailwind.

        Tens of millions of adults

        Adult hearing loss in the United States alone affects tens of millions of people, and only a minority use hearing aids today — held back by cost, prescription requirements, clinic visits, and stigma.

        The OTC regulatory shift

        The FDA's creation of an over-the-counter hearing-aid category opened a non-prescription path through pharmacies, mass-market retailers, and direct e-commerce. That shift is the single largest structural change to the hearing-aid market in a generation.

        Where InnerScope sits

        At the intersection of OTC regulation, established retail relationships now operated through OTCHealth, and a digital-health and teleaudiology technology base inherited from the iHEAR Medical acquisition. Family-clinic operating instinct still shapes which products get built.
